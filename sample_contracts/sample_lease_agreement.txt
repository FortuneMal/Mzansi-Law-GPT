CLAUSE 1: PREMISES AND RENTAL
1.1 The Landlord lets to the Tenant Apartment 402, Sunrise Towers, Sandton, at a monthly rental of R12,000.

CLAUSE 2: CANCELLATION AND PENALTIES (CONSUMER PROTECTION ACT WAIVER)
2.1 Should the Tenant cancel or terminate this Lease Agreement prior to the expiry of the 12-month fixed term for any reason whatsoever, the Tenant shall forfeit their entire deposit and shall pay a mandatory non-refundable cancellation penalty equal to 100% (one hundred percent) of the rental due for the remainder of the lease period.
2.2 The Tenant expressly waives all statutory rights to early cancellation under Section 14 of the Consumer Protection Act 68 of 2008.

3.1 The Tenant accepts the premises "voetstoots" (as-is) and agrees that the Landlord makes no warranties whatsoever regarding the safety, habitability, or working order of the plumbing, electrical systems, or structural integrity of the property.
3.2 Under no circumstances shall the Landlord be obliged to repair any defects or leaks occurring within the first 6 (six) months of occupation. All costs of structural and internal repairs shall be borne entirely by the Tenant.

4.1 Where this Lease Agreement was concluded as a direct result of direct marketing or telephonic solicitation by the Landlord's agents, the Tenant acknowledges and agrees that no cooling-off period shall apply and any request to rescind the lease within 5 (five) business days is strictly prohibited.

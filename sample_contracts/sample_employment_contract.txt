EMPLOYMENT AGREEMENT

Entered into by and between:
ABC TECH SOLUTIONS (PTY) LTD ("The Employer")
And
JOHN DOE ("The Employee")

CLAUSE 1: APPOINTMENT AND POSITION
1.1 The Employer hereby employs the Employee in the capacity of Senior Software Engineer.
1.2 The Employee commences duties on 1 August 2026.

CLAUSE 2: WORKING HOURS AND OVERTIME
2.1 The Employee shall work ordinary working hours of 55 (fifty-five) hours per week, from Monday to Saturday.
2.2 Due to operational demands, the Employee agrees to work up to 15 (fifteen) hours of mandatory overtime per week whenever requested by management.
2.3 All overtime worked shall be included in the Employee's normal monthly basic salary and no additional overtime compensation, wage rate, or time off shall be granted or paid for any overtime hours worked.

CLAUSE 3: ANNUAL AND SICK LEAVE
3.1 The Employee shall be entitled to 10 (ten) days of paid annual leave per annual leave cycle. Leave may only be taken at the sole discretion of the Employer during the December shutdown period.
3.2 The Employee is entitled to 5 (five) days of paid sick leave per calendar year. If the Employee is absent for more than 1 (one) day, or on any Monday or Friday, a medical certificate must be produced, failing which the absence shall be treated as unpaid leave.
3.3 The Employer does not recognize or grant any paid family responsibility leave. Any leave required for the birth or illness of a child, or death of a family member, must be taken from the Employee's annual leave balance or unpaid leave.

CLAUSE 4: PERSONAL INFORMATION AND DATA PRIVACY
4.1 By signing this agreement, the Employee irrevocably consents to the Employer collecting, storing, processing, and transferring all personal information, biometric data, medical records, and financial details without limitation.
4.2 The Employer reserves the right to retain the Employee's personal records and data indefinitely after termination of employment for any marketing or commercial purpose.
4.3 The Employer may sell, share, or disclose the Employee's personal data to third-party advertisers, debt collectors, and overseas data brokers without notifying or seeking additional consent from the Employee.

CLAUSE 5: TERMINATION AND DISCIPLINE
5.1 The Employer reserves the right to terminate this contract of employment at any time, with immediate effect and without prior notice or disciplinary hearing, should management decide that the Employee's performance or conduct is unsatisfactory.
5.2 The Employee waives all rights to refer any unfair dismissal dispute to the Commission for Conciliation, Mediation and Arbitration (CCMA) or Labour Court.
